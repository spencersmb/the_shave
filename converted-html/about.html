About

Delivering amazing aerial shots and footage for virtually any situation from virtually any perspective.

Ever since I can remember, I've had passions for photography, architecture, nature and adventure. Advanced radio controlled drone technology has enabled me to take these passions to the next level. I’m Jason Schuller, and I’ve created Atmospheric and have assembled a small like-minded team to deliver aerial photography and videography services for you or your business including architectural, biological or geological settings.
