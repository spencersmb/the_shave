Wild Stallion

A strong and bold cut for free spirits.

The Style

This cut was inspired by Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventure. It embodies the free spirit of a horse running through the sandy beaches etc.Introducing Playdium, the digital jukebox that delivers a new kind of entertainment experience.

Product

Use: Keune Forte Gel, Keune Moulding Paste.
Apply: Firmly add gel to wet hair. Air dry and finish with a flexible wax or pomade that also adds texture.

Price

$45.00

Hair Length

Medium

Style Tip

This hair cut is using the scissor-over-comb technique all the way from the hairline to the corners of the head.

Face Type

Square

Style Tip
